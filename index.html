1.0 Executive Summary

Reflective Diagnostics provides static analysis instruments for high-stakes governance. Unlike runtime dashboards that introduce vendor dependency and volatility, we produce immutable artifacts designed for archival and evidentiary use.

2.0 What It Detects

The Structural Assurance module analyzes governance documentation to identify logical voids rather than content quality. It detects:

  • Unsupported claims lacking backward-chaining to evidence.
  • Missing internal substantiation for strategic directives.
  • Breaks in the traceability chain between policy and procedure.

3.0 Target Audience

This format is specifically engineered for:

  • Legal Counsel: Creating defensible records of due diligence.
  • Audit Committees: verifying structural integrity of internal controls.
  • Investment Committees: Assessing governance maturity pre-deployment.
